# Change: Clinic Access Ledger

## Why

The clinic is currently a municipal site with observation-only readouts. The next slice needs public care to become one small institutional access surface tied to existing local suspicion and wage-record pressure, without adding health, medicine, doctors, appointments, factions, or public-works simulation.

## What Changes

- Add a clinic-local access ledger state that appears only when an existing worker local-suspicion/wage record exists.
- Surface the flagged clinic ledger through ordinary clinic inspection, `SPACE` Debugger scan, and AI playtest target detail.
- Let `G` Interference Torch toggle one reversible clinic access spoof state.
- Preserve clinic non-enterable behavior and leave local suspicion, wage spoofing, route blockage, workplace audit traces, and broader systems unchanged.
