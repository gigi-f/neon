# Requirements

### Requirement: EconomicComponent
Every citizen NPC SHALL carry an `EconomicComponent` with three fields:
- `credits`: float ‚Äî current wallet balance; randomized at spawn to [50, 150)
- `employer`: Entity ‚Äî building entity that employs this citizen; MAX_ENTITIES = unemployed
- `daily_wage`: float ‚Äî credits earned per game-day; randomized at spawn to [5, 25) if employed, 0.0 if not

The component is declared in `components.h` under the `// ‚îÄ‚îÄ L3 Economic ‚îÄ‚îÄ` banner.

#### Scenario: Component Assignment
- **WHEN** a citizen entity is spawned by `AmbientSpawnSystem::spawnCitizens()`
- **THEN** it SHALL receive an `EconomicComponent` with random credits and employer assignment (see Requirement: Employment Assignment)

---

### Requirement: EmployerComponent
A random ~30% of building entities generated by `WorldGeneration` SHALL receive an `EmployerComponent`:
- `capacity`: int ‚Äî max employees [3, 8]
- `employee_count`: int ‚Äî starts at 0, incremented as citizens are employed

`EmployerComponent` is assigned during world generation after building entities are created.

#### Scenario: Building becomes employer
- **WHEN** a building entity is created during world generation
- **THEN** there is a 30% chance it receives an `EmployerComponent` with random capacity

---

### Requirement: Employment Assignment
During `AmbientSpawnSystem::spawnCitizens()`, after creating a citizen entity:
- Query all entities with `EmployerComponent` where `employee_count < capacity`
- Find the nearest one within 400 WU of the citizen's spawn position
- **If found**: set `eco.employer = building`, `eco.daily_wage = random [5, 25)`, increment `building.employee_count`
- **If not found**: citizen is unemployed (`employer = MAX_ENTITIES`, `daily_wage = 0.0`)

#### Scenario: Nearby employer found
- **WHEN** a citizen spawns and a building with available capacity is within 400 WU
- **THEN** the citizen SHALL be employed, with daily_wage assigned and employer's employee_count incremented

#### Scenario: No employer available
- **WHEN** no employer building is within 400 WU or all nearby employers are at capacity
- **THEN** the citizen SHALL be unemployed (employer = MAX_ENTITIES, daily_wage = 0)

---

### Requirement: WageSystem
`WageSystem::update(registry, time_scale)` SHALL run at the L3 tick gate (~1 Hz real-time).

For each entity with `EconomicComponent`:
- If `employer != MAX_ENTITIES`:
  - If `!registry.alive(employer)`: set `employer = MAX_ENTITIES`, `daily_wage = 0` (employer destroyed)
  - Else: `credits += daily_wage * time_scale / 1440.0f`  ‚Üê game-minutes per real second / game-minutes per day
  - Clamp `credits` to [0, 9999]

#### Wage formula derivation
- `time_scale` = game-minutes per real second (default 2.0)
- L3 fires at ~1 Hz real-time (every 60 frames at 60 fps)
- Each L3 tick advances ~`time_scale` game-minutes
- Day = 1440 game-minutes ‚Üí `wage_increment = daily_wage * time_scale / 1440`

#### Scenario: Employed citizen earns wages
- **WHEN** a citizen is employed and WageSystem runs
- **THEN** their credits SHALL increase by `daily_wage * time_scale / 1440` per tick, reaching `daily_wage` after one game-day

#### Scenario: Employer entity is destroyed
- **WHEN** a citizen's employer entity is destroyed (e.g., entity deletion)
- **THEN** the citizen SHALL become unemployed on the next WageSystem tick

---

### Requirement: Financial Stress ‚Üí PAD Feedback
`WageSystem::update()` SHALL apply a PAD penalty for financially distressed citizens:
- **WHEN** `credits < 20.0` AND `employer == MAX_ENTITIES` (unemployed and broke)
- **THEN** `cog.pleasure = max(-1.0, cog.pleasure - 0.03)` per L3 tick

This feeds L2 behavior: distressed citizens have lower pleasure ‚Üí higher arousal ‚Üí more urgent hunger/thirst desires.

#### Scenario: Broke and unemployed citizen becomes anxious
- **WHEN** a citizen has credits below 20 and no employer
- **THEN** their pleasure SHALL decrease each L3 tick, raising arousal via CognitiveSystem feedback

---

### Requirement: Infrastructure Unchanged
Biology, cognitive, schedule, GDI, and all L2 systems SHALL NOT be modified.
`ConsumableSystem` SHALL continue to provide food/water at zero credit cost (market pricing is a future spec).
The `tick_l3()` gate in `main.cpp` currently runs only the debug HUD ‚Äî `WageSystem::update()` SHALL be added to the same gate block.
